# Introduction
Concept Design Management (CDM) is a collaborative design workspace in the IM suite. With concepts, you can convert product ideas into detailed prototypes that are ready for production. 

## Audience
This guide is intended for product architects, managers, and executives who are involved in product design and conceptualization.

## About this guide
This document describes CDM in the IM suite. For information on requirements and proposals, see {links to IM product guides}.
